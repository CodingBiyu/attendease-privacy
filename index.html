1. Information We Collect

  • Camera Data: Used for QR code scanning only. Not stored or shared.
  • Location Data: Collected at the time of attendance to verify presence.
  • User Data: Name, university ID, and attendance records.
  • Device Info: Device type, OS version, crash logs for debugging only.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Verify physical presence during attendance using QR and GPS.
  • Maintain attendance logs securely.
  • Notify users about successful check-ins or issues.
  • Improve app stability and performance using crash data.

3. Data Storage & Retention

  • All user data is stored securely in university or app admin databases.
  • No data is shared with third parties or used for advertising.
  • Data is retained only for attendance record purposes.

4. Data Security

  • Secure HTTPS communication between app and server.
  • Authentication to access admin or student dashboards.
  • Limited access to stored attendance data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with any third party except:

  • University staff or admin for academic records
  • Legal authorities if required by law

6. Your Privacy Choices

  • You may revoke location or camera access via device settings.
  • Request data removal by emailing us at attendease.support@gmail.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Latest version will always be available in-app or online.
